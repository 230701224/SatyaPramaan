Stage 3 — Cross-Document consistency

Multi-Document Reconciliation Matrix

Individual documents are rarely manipulated in isolation. SatyaPramaan maps relationships between documents (Salary Slip ↔ Bank Statement ↔ ITR Filing). We automatically cross-reference applicant names, PAN card numbers, and verify that monthly salary credits listed on paycheck slips align with deposits in banking ledgers to catch multi-document fraud rings.

Stage 4 — LLM-Powered Risk Insight

Plain-English Explanations

Our pipeline translates complex technical data outputs into natural, non-black-box underwriting feedback. We supply clear explanations detailing where the document fails compliance along with explainable audit trails.
